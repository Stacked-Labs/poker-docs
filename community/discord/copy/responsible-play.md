# Responsible play

Real-money poker involves real risk. Most players have winning weeks and losing weeks. A small number of players don't. This channel is for those of you in the second group, and for anyone who thinks they might be.

## A short self-check

If any of the following are true, it's worth pausing:

- You're playing with money you can't afford to lose.
- You're chasing losses — playing bigger, longer, or more often after a bad session to "win it back."
- You're hiding how much you're playing or how much you've lost from people close to you.
- Poker is interfering with your sleep, your work, your relationships, or your finances.
- You've tried to cut back and couldn't.

None of these on their own mean you have a problem. All of them together usually do.

## What you can do, right now

- **Step away from real money.** Stacked has Free Play tables that play exactly like real-money tables, without the financial risk. Switching to Free Play for a stretch is a real option, not a participation trophy.
- **Set a stop-loss before you sit.** A buy-in is the unit; pick a number of buy-ins you're willing to lose in a session and quit when you hit it.
- **Use the platform.** When you leave a real-money table, your stack returns to your wallet. If you want to make it harder to come back, withdraw to a wallet you don't have on the same device.
- **Talk to someone.** A friend, a partner, anyone. Quiet doesn't help.

## Help that exists outside Stacked

These are independent of Stacked. They will not ask you to log into anything, and you don't need to mention us.

- **National Council on Problem Gambling (US)** — call or text **1-800-GAMBLER** (1-800-426-2537). 24/7, confidential.
- **GamCare (UK)** — [gamcare.org.uk](https://www.gamcare.org.uk), free helpline at 0808 8020 133.
- **Gambling Therapy (international)** — [gamblingtherapy.org](https://www.gamblingtherapy.org), online support across many countries and languages.
- **Gamblers Anonymous** — [gamblersanonymous.org](https://www.gamblersanonymous.org), meeting locator worldwide.

If you're in another country and not sure where to start, Gambling Therapy will route you.

## What Stacked will do if you ask

We don't run a self-exclusion list (you don't have an account to exclude — we don't have one to put you on). What we can do is help you remove yourself from this server, mute the channels that push real-money games, and route you toward Free Play only. Open a ticket in `🛟-support` with the topic *responsible play* and we'll handle it without making it a conversation you don't want to have.

You're not in trouble for asking.
