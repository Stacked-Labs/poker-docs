# House rules

Stacked is built in the open. These rules keep it that way. They apply in every channel, every thread, every voice channel, and every DM that started on this server.

**1. Be a good table.** No harassment, slurs, hate speech, doxxing, or threats. Disagree with the hand, not the player.

**2. The team will never DM you first.** If you get a DM claiming to be from Stacked, it isn't. Report it in `#support`. We will never ask for your seed phrase, your private keys, or a "verification" signature outside of the app itself.

**3. There is no Stacked token.** No airdrop, no presale, no "claim rewards" page. Anyone telling you otherwise — including a fake version of this server — is trying to steal from you.

**4. No spam, no shilling, no NSFW.** No invite links to other servers without team approval. No promoting other projects, tokens, or platforms. No NSFW content anywhere on the server.

**5. Settle money on-chain, settle disputes in `#support`.** Your stack is in the contract, not on Discord. If a settlement looks wrong, open a ticket. Don't take it out on other players.

---

*Breaking these can mean a warning, a timeout, or a ban depending on how bad and how often. Mods log every action — we don't act on vibes.*

*Stacked is 18+. Real-money tables involve real risk. If poker is affecting your life, your relationships, or your finances, see `#responsible-play`.*

*By staying in this server, you agree to these rules and to [Discord's Community Guidelines](https://discord.com/guidelines).*
