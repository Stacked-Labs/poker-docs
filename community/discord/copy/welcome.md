# Welcome to Stacked

Stacked is a poker platform where every table is created by a player and every real-money hand settles to USDC on Base. The cards run off-chain; the money runs on-chain.

This server is where players find seats, hosts find players, and the team is reachable in the open.

## Start here

**1.** Read `📜-rules`. Five rules, no fine print.

**2.** Run the onboarding prompt below this message — three questions, takes 30 seconds. It unlocks the rest of the server.

**3.** Pick where you're going next:

- 🆓 **New to poker?** → `🎓-poker-basics` → grab a seat in `🆓-free-play-tables`.
- 🪙 **New to wallets?** → `🪪-connect-a-wallet` → `🪙-usdc-and-funding`.
- 💵 **Here to play for real?** → `💵-real-money-tables` and `🎲-find-a-game`.
- 🛠️ **Want to host your own table?** → open a ticket in `🛟-support` to apply. Hosts earn 25% of the rake at their tables.

## How help works here

- **Questions about the game, money, contracts, anything:** `🛟-support`. Opens a private ticket with the team.
- **Bugs:** `🐛-bug-reports`.
- **Feature ideas:** `💡-feature-requests`.
- **Security-sensitive reports** (vulnerabilities in the contracts or the backend): open a `🛟-support` ticket and mark it as security. Don't post details in public.

## Two things we'll repeat a lot

- The team will **never** DM you first. Anyone claiming to be Stacked support in your DMs is a scam.
- There is **no Stacked token**. No airdrop, no presale, no "claim" page. Anyone telling you otherwise is trying to steal from you.

Good luck at the tables.

— The Stacked team

## Community Onboarding questions

> Paste these into `Server Settings → Onboarding`. Three questions, multi-select where it makes sense. Each answer toggles cosmetic roles and surfaces relevant channels.

**Question 1 (single-select). Where are you coming from?**

- 🎰 **I play poker.** New to crypto / wallets. → surfaces `🪪-connect-a-wallet`, `🪙-usdc-and-funding`, `🧰-self-custody-help`.
- 🪙 **I'm crypto-native.** New to poker. → surfaces `🎓-poker-basics`, `🆓-free-play-101`, `🆓-free-play-tables`.
- 🃏 **I do both.** → surfaces `💵-real-money-tables`, `📋-hand-reviews`.
- 🛠️ **I want to host my own tables.** → surfaces `🛠️-host-lounge` (preview), `🛟-support`.

**Question 2 (multi-select). What do you want to be pinged about?**

- 🎰 New cash games opening up → grants `🎰 Cash Game`.
- 🏆 Tournaments (when they ship) → grants `🏆 Tournaments`.
- 🆓 Free Play nights → grants `🆓 Free Play`.
- 📢 Big announcements only → grants `🔔 All Announcements`.
- 🔕 Nothing. I'll check in. → no roles granted.

**Question 3 (single-select). What language do you mostly chat in?**

- 🇬🇧 English → grants `🌐 EN`.
- 🇪🇸 Spanish → grants `🌐 ES`.
- 🇧🇷 Portuguese → grants `🌐 PT`.
- 🇫🇷 French → grants `🌐 FR`.
- 🇰🇷 Korean → grants `🌐 KR`.
- 🇹🇷 Turkish → grants `🌐 TR`.
- 🌍 Something else.

> Question 3 surfaces `🌐-international` once that forum has a post for the chosen language. Until then it's purely a tag.
