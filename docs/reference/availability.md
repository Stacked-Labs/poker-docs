# Where Stacked is available

:::info

This page is being finalized. We're working through the geographic availability list with our legal team. Until that's locked, treat the information here as preliminary.

:::

Stacked is designed to be permissionless — connect a wallet, sit at a table, play. There's no KYC, no email signup, no account verification. That said, some jurisdictions block access to online poker platforms by law, and Stacked respects those restrictions.

## Blocked regions

_The specific list of blocked regions will appear here once finalized._

If access is restricted in your region, Stacked may block sign-in from your network connection. We won't take steps beyond that — no identity collection, no probabilistic geofencing.

## Sanctioned jurisdictions

Stacked does not operate in jurisdictions subject to comprehensive US, UK, or EU sanctions. _Specific list pending._

## Your local laws

You're responsible for knowing whether real-money online poker is permitted where you are. Stacked does not provide legal advice and isn't a substitute for checking your local rules.
