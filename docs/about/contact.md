# Contact and community

Stacked is built in the open with the people playing on it. Here's where to find us and how to get in touch.

## Community channels

- **Discord** — the main place. Conversation about hands, hosting, feature requests, bug reports.
- **Telegram** — for players who prefer it.
- **X (Twitter)** — announcements and updates.

_Links live in the navbar at the top of every page._

## Reporting an issue

For bugs, vulnerabilities, or anything that looks wrong, reach out in Discord and tag a team member. Security-sensitive reports — vulnerabilities in the contracts or the backend — should be sent as a direct message rather than posted publicly.

## Press and partnerships

For press, integrations, or partnership inquiries, reach out through any of the channels above and we'll route you to the right person.
